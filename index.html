Bigfoot
An ape-like creature said to roam the forests of North America. Reports put bigfoot at around 6-15 feet. Bigfoot was first spotted by a journalist by the name of Andrew Genzoli who got a call from local loggers about mysterious huge footprints. However, bigfoot's origins span even further back in time, as many indigenous people tell stories of huge harry men.

My Findings
After conducting multiple of my own bigfoot hunts, I can conclude that this hairy beast is real. I have collected casts of huge footprints in the ground, and I have even recorded audio of weird growling that couldn't have come from any other animal. Conspiracy Confirmed.

Loch Ness Monster
A large marine creature rumored by many to inhabit Loch Ness, Scotland. Information about this legendary beast is not well know. However, many attribute it to sea-like dinosaurs in terms of size, and perhaps, origin. The first sightings of the Loch Ness monster date back to 565 AD. The first photograph was taken in 1933, but since then many more have been taken. 

My Findings
After taking out a boat out on the lake, I can confidently conclude that this mystical beast is real! I was able to record multiple videos; however, my camera was knocked overboard when a huge wave almost tipped me over. I almost came face to face with the monster, and it is exactly how others describe. Conspiracy confirmed.
